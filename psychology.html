CUET (Common University Entrance Test)

Introduction

The Common University Entrance Test (CUET) is a standardized exam for admission into various undergraduate programs at central and other participating universities across India.

Exam Structure & Syllabus

  • Computer-Based Test (CBT): Conducted online with multiple-choice questions.
  • Sections:
    • Language Test
    • Domain-Specific Subjects (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, etc.)
    • General Test (Logical reasoning, analytical skills, and general awareness)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Class 12 or equivalent with at least 50% marks (varies by university).
  • SC/ST/OBC candidates may be eligible for relaxation.

Participating Universities

  • Delhi University
  • Banaras Hindu University
  • Aligarh Muslim University
  • University of Hyderabad
  • Many others across India

Preparation Tips

  • Thoroughly review NCERT textbooks.
  • Practice previous year question papers.
  • Take mock tests for better time management.
