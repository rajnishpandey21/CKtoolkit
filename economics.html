CUET (Common University Entrance Test) for B.A. (Hons.) in Economics

Introduction

The Common University Entrance Test (CUET) is a national-level entrance exam conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to undergraduate and postgraduate courses in central universities, state universities, and deemed universities.

Exam Structure

The CUET for B.A. (Hons.) in Economics consists of three sections:

  • Section I: Language Test (13 languages to choose from)
  • Section II: Domain-Specific Subject (Economics)
  • Section III: General Test (Comprehension, Quantitative Reasoning, Logical and Analytical Reasoning)

Paper Pattern

  • Section I - Language Test:
    • 40 questions
    • 4 marks each
    • Total Marks: 160
  • Section II - Domain-Specific Subject (Economics):
    • 50 questions
    • 4 marks each
    • Total Marks: 200
  • Section III - General Test:
    • 75 questions
    • 4 marks each
    • Total Marks: 300

Marking Scheme

  • Each correct answer carries 4 marks.
  • No negative marking for incorrect answers.

Other Information

  • The CUET is conducted in a computer-based test (CBT) mode.
  • The duration of the exam is 3 hours.
  • Candidates can choose to appear for the exam in English or Hindi.
  • Application Fee: Rs. 650 for General & OBC candidates, Rs. 550 for SC, ST, and PwD candidates.
  • For more details, visit the official CUET website.

CUET (Common University Entrance Test) for B.Sc. (Hons.) in Economics

Introduction

The Common University Entrance Test (CUET) is a national-level entrance exam conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission to undergraduate courses in central universities and other participating institutions across India.

CUET is mandatory for admission to B.Sc. (Hons.) in Economics in several universities, including:

  • University of Delhi
  • Banaras Hindu University
  • University of Mumbai
  • Calcutta University
  • University of Hyderabad
  • University of Madras
  • Aligarh Muslim University

Structure of the Exam

The CUET for B.Sc. (Hons.) in Economics consists of three sections:

  • Section I: Language Test (English, Hindi, or any other language)
  • Section II: Domain-Specific Subject (Economics)
  • Section III: General Test (GK, Current Affairs, Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning)

Paper Pattern

  • Section I - Language Test: 40 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
  • Section II - Economics: 50 MCQs
  • Section III - General Test: 75 MCQs

Marking Scheme

  • Each question carries 4 marks.
  • No negative marking for incorrect answers.

CUET (Common University Entrance Test) for B.A. in Business Economics

Exam Structure

The CUET for B.A. in Business Economics consists of three sections:

  • Section 1: Language Test (English or Hindi)
  • Section 2: Domain-Specific Subject (Business Economics)
  • Section 3: General Test (Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, and General Awareness)

Paper Pattern

  • Section 1 - Language Test (English or Hindi):
    • 50 questions
    • 45 minutes
    • Multiple-choice questions
  • Section 2 - Business Economics:
    • 50 questions
    • 45 minutes
    • Multiple-choice questions
  • Section 3 - General Test (Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, and General Awareness):
    • 75 questions
    • 60 minutes
    • Multiple-choice questions

Marking Scheme

  • Each question carries 4 marks.
  • No negative marking for incorrect answers.
