NCHMCT-JEE: Your Gateway to Hospitality Management

Introduction

The National Council for Hotel Management and Catering Technology Joint Entrance Examination (NCHMCT-JEE) is a national-level entrance exam for admission to hospitality and hotel management programs in India.

Exam Structure

  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Mode: Computer-based test (CBT)
  • Sections:
    • Numerical Ability & Analytical Aptitude
    • Reasoning & Logical Deduction
    • General Knowledge & Current Affairs
    • English Language
    • Aptitude for Service Sector

Eligibility Criteria

  • Qualification: 10+2 or equivalent
  • Age Limit: Maximum 25 years (General/OBC), 28 years (SC/ST)

Top Colleges Accepting NCHMCT-JEE

  • Institute of Hotel Management (IHM), Delhi
  • IHM Mumbai
  • IHM Bangalore
  • IHM Kolkata
  • IHM Hyderabad
  • Other affiliated hospitality institutes

Preparation Tips

  • Focus on English and Service Sector Aptitude
  • Practice numerical ability and logical reasoning questions
  • Stay updated with current affairs
  • Take mock tests to improve speed and accuracy

Career Prospects

A degree in hospitality management opens doors to careers in hotels, airlines, event management, cruise lines, and more.
